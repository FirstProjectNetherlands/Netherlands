A C T I V T I E S

Discover the best activities and things to do in the Netherlands. Go cycling from one end of the country to the other, or go for walks in the long stretches of forests here, or just laze around in a yacht on a warm sunny day. There is just so much to do. And then there is shopping too. A visit to the local markets is a must!

CYCLING

Holland is the ultimate destination for cycling thanks to its flat landscape, mild climate, short distances and wonderful infrastructure. It's safe, easy and great fun! More than 37,000 kilometres of bicycle paths are waiting for you throughout the country. So if you want to see more of Holland, one of the best ways to do so is by bicycle.

SALING

Sailing through Holland, you will experience the characteristic landscape with its beautiful nature. You will sail from village to village, stopping for a break or overnight stay. With so much water and lakes, you can always find a spot to go sailing, a boat rental company, or a sailing school.

HIKING

The Netherlands can be explored excellently not only by bicycle, but also on foot. Natural oases such as the Hoge Veluwe National Park, South Limburg or the Arkemheen-Eemland National Park form unique backdrops for unforgettable excursions

KITESURFING

Looking for action and adventure? Kitesurfing might be just the thing for you! This sport is a mix of several sports, such as windsurfing, paragliding and snowboarding. A kite and a board allow you to fly across the water and jump to great heights.

BOATING

Boating is a way of life in the Netherlands, where water occupies a significant proportion of the country’s surface area. Holland offers visitors a feast of unique and amazingly varied cruising surprisingly close to our own south and east coasts.

SKY DIVING

If you feel like an extraordinary experience while holidaying on the peaceful Wadden islands, go skydiving at Parachute centrum Texel. Here, professional instructors will help you make the jump of a lifetime in a truly stunning environment.

SHOPPING

The Netherlands can be a paradise for the shopping enthusiast. From brands and designer outlets to local artworks and products, there is something here for every kind of shopper. Shop till you drop at the Kalverstraat, the 9 Straatjes, Batavia Stad and Roermond.

WEEKLY MARKETS

As most Dutch towns developed around daily or weekly markets, it is still exceptionally easy to find awesome, stall-based shopping opportunities in the Netherlands. Many of these markets have taken place on the same spot for centuries, whilst other newer additions occur within stunning, modern locations
